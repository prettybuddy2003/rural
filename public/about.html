About Us

RuralCare Innovators LLP has been started with the aim of taking the advancement of utmost technologies like Internet of Things(IOT), Artificial Intelligence, Robotics and Industrial automation with reasonable costto the rural villages of India therby making the villages self sufficient in technologies and creating the villages as production hub . The RuralCare Innovators LLP has been started with the aim to stop migration of village people towards the cities and attract the cities towards the villages by using the already existing resources therebyrealize the dreams of visionary Leaders of India. The Company dreams includes the agriculture, Health care and smart home of Rural people.
